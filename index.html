ABOUT

At (دار الارقم و الدعوة الاسلا مية) DAARUL ARQAM COLLEGE we are concerned about giving muslim children the right to islamic and general education. our school is Bi-lingual(Arabic and English) which in essence will earn our student a defendable (thanwiyat) certificate as far as arabic education is concerned, SSS3 certificate, complete Quran memorization certificate and other islamic moral values, ettiquettes, culture, are like a child's play with us at DAARUL ARQAM COLLEGE.
